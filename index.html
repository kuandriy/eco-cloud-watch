Understand the Environmental Impact of Your Cloud Environment

Problem Identification

Cloud infrastructure is growing rapidly, but many users are unaware of how their usage translates into energy consumption and carbon emissions. Data centers consume significant energy, and over-provisioning resources lead to wasted energy and increased costs.

Value Proposition

Eco Cloudwatch offers a simplified, actionable view of your cloud environment's carbon emissions. Our platform helps you optimize your cloud setup, reduce costs, and contribute to a sustainable future.
